## Professional Profile

Security Engineer and founder focused on making enterprise-grade security accessible through cloud-native, marketplace-deployable products. I build and operate [HailBytes](https://hailbytes.com), where we ship two flagship products on AWS and Azure Marketplace: HailBytes ASM (Attack Surface Management) and HailBytes SAT (Security Awareness Training), both running on a bring-your-own-cloud architecture so customers keep their data in their own tenant.

My work spans the full stack of a security product company: Terraform and Packer pipelines for multi-cloud deployment, GRC and compliance (SOC 2, FedRAMP, HECVAT, NIST CSF, ISO 27001), and the offensive tooling that informs it. When I am not on the security side, I run [Lost Rabbit Digital](https://lostrabbit.digital), shipping games on Steam and building consumer software.

## Value Proposition

- **Product-grade security.** I turn frameworks and controls into deployable products, not just documentation.
- **Cloud-native by default.** Marketplace listings, BYOC architecture, and IaC pipelines that customers can stand up in their own cloud.
- **Builder mindset.** Comfortable across security engineering, DevSecOps, and full product delivery from infrastructure to go-to-market.
